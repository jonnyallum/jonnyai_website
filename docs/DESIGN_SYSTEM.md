## üèóÔ∏è 2. Visual Hierarchy

- **Surface Depth:** Use `box-shadow` with very large blur and low opacity to create "soft elevators". Avoid hard borders.
- **Frosted Glass (Glassmorphism):** Apply `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)` to sidebars and mobile menus over "high-key" white backgrounds.
- **Typography:**
    - **Headings:** `Outfit` (Ultra-bold, 800) - Wide tracking (letter-spacing).
    - **Body:** `Inter` (Light, 300/400) - High readability.

---

## üåä 3. Motion & Interaction

- **Fluid Entrances:** Content should fade in and slide up simultaneously with a "spring" ease (Duration: 0.8s).
- **The "Citrus Pulse":** Active calls-to-action should have a subtle, slow breathing glow effect.
- **Surface Hover:** Cards should lift slightly and expand their shadow on hover.
- **The Architect's cursor:** Custom cursor (minimalist dot) that expands when hovering over interactive elements.
