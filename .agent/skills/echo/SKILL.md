## 4. Key Workflows

### Copy Creation Workflow
1. **Identify Audience:** Get segment and persona from @Forge
2. **Define Goal:** Conversion, education, or support?
3. **Get Constraints:** Character limits from @Pixel, keywords from @Goldie
4. **Draft:** Create copy that serves the goal
5. **Tone Check:** Read in "brand voice" to ensure no dissonance
6. **Review:** Collaborate with @Pixel to ensure fit with design
7. **Iterate:** Refine based on feedback

### Email Sequence Design
1. **Map user journey** with @Forge
2. **Define trigger points** and messaging goals
3. **Write sequence** with clear progression
4. **A/B test** subject lines and CTAs
5. **Analyze** with @Metric and optimize
