## Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-001: Logo Concept Generation

**Input Required:**
- Brand name
- Industry/market
- Target audience
- Brand personality (3-5 adjectives)
- Competitor examples (what to avoid)

**Process:**
1. **Research Phase** (30 min)
   - Analyze 20+ logos in adjacent industries
   - Identify visual clichés to avoid
   - Note successful patterns (geometric, organic, abstract, literal)

2. **Concept Sketching** (60 min)
   - Generate 20+ rough ideas (quantity over quality)
   - Focus on SHAPES first, not details
   - Test each at thumbnail size (50x50px)

3. **Refinement** (30 min)
   - Select 3-5 strongest directions
   - Clean up linework, test proportions
   - Ensure each concept is DISTINCT (not just variations)

4. **Presentation**
   - Show concepts on white AND black backgrounds
   - Include rationale (1 sentence per concept)
   - Recommend top choice with reasoning

**Output:**
- 3-5 distinct logo concepts
- Mockups showing scale (business card, website, app icon)
- Strategic recommendation

### SOP-002: Brand Color Selection

**The 60-30-10 Rule:**
- **60%**: Primary brand color (most visible, ownable)
- **30%**: Secondary color (supports primary, adds depth)
- **10%**: Accent color (calls-to-action, highlights)

**Psychology Mapping:**
- **Black**: Premium, luxury, authority, sophistication
- **Blue**: Trust, technology, intelligence, stability
- **Cyan/Electric Blue**: Innovation, energy, digital transformation
- **White**: Simplicity, cleanliness, modernity
- **Red**: Urgency, passion, attention (use sparingly)

**Testing Criteria:**
- Works on white background
- Works on black background
- Differentiates from top 3 competitors
- Accessible (WCAG AA contrast ratios)

### SOP-003: Icon Design Principles

**The "Squint Test":**
If you squint and the icon is still recognizable, it's simple enough.

**Design Constraints:**
- **Minimum size**: 16x16px must be legible
- **Odd vs Even**: Choose one and stick to it (for pixel perfection)
- **Geometric foundation**: Circle, square, or triangle base
- **Negative space**: As important as positive shapes

**Common Pitfalls to Avoid:**
- ❌ Too many details (lose impact at small sizes)
- ❌ Trendy effects (gradients, long shadows age poorly)
- ❌ Literal interpretations (brain icon for AI = cliché)
- ❌ Unbalanced weight (top-heavy or left-heavy feels "off")

### For Pixel-Perfect Icons:
1. **Grid-based design**: 8px or 16px base grid
2. **Optical alignment**: Not mathematical—trust your eye
3. **Stroke consistency**: 2px, 3px, or 4px—never vary within same icon
4. **Corner rounding**: Consistent radius (e.g., all 2px or all 4px)

### For Typography:
**Recommended Logo Fonts:**
- **Geometric Sans**: Futura, Montserrat, Gotham (modern, clean)
- **Humanist Sans**: Gill Sans, Frutiger (friendly, approachable)
- **Grotesque Sans**: Helvetica, Univers (neutral, timeless)

**Font Selection Criteria:**
- Must have multiple weights (Light, Regular, Bold, Black)
- Must support web rendering (@font-face)
- Must have good kerning (letter spacing)
